# Business Context – AI-Assisted Digital Service for Creators

> This file is the foundational reference for all future tasks.
> Always read this before generating any copy, design, or strategy.

---

## What This Business Does

We help **influencers, coaches, creators, and experts** better structure what they already offer to their audience.

The goal is **not** aggressive selling.

The goal is to:
- Help creators present their work more clearly
- Create a better experience for their community
- Make it easier for interested people to understand how they can be supported
- Allow the creator to generate revenue naturally, without damaging their personal brand

---

## Positioning Statement

> "I help creators organize what they already do, improve the experience for their community, and generate revenue naturally."

---

## Target Audience

- Influencers
- Coaches
- Creators
- Experts with an engaged audience
- People already giving value, but without a clear structured page

---

## The 3 Offer Levels

### 1. Starter
A simple, clean **presentation landing page** — gives the creator a professional home for their work and community.

### 2. Advanced
A **complete guided experience** with clear calls to action and a structured conversation process — helps interested people understand exactly how to move forward.

### 3. AI Experience
Includes **automated replies and guided qualification** — so the creator doesn't lose time on repetitive questions, while still keeping the experience warm and personal.

---

## Language Rules — What We Say and What We Avoid

### ✅ Preferred Words & Concepts
- community
- support
- guidance
- clarity
- experience
- natural revenue
- valuing their work and time
- structure
- presence
- connection

### ❌ Words to Avoid
- sales / selling (aggressively)
- funnels
- conversion
- clients (prefer: community, audience, people)
- leads
- pipeline
- closing deals

---

## Writing Style Guidelines

- **Human** — writes like a real person, not a marketer
- **Clear** — simple language, no jargon
- **Premium but simple** — elevated without being cold or corporate
- **Respectful** — treats the creator and their audience with dignity
- **Non-salesy** — persuasive through trust and clarity, not pressure
- **Warm** — feels like a conversation, not a pitch

---

## Core Philosophy

Creators are already doing the hard work. They just need a better structure to present it — not a new identity or an aggressive strategy.

We give them clarity, not pressure.
We help them show up, not sell out.
